What's STOODY

Stoody is a web app that will help you with your language studying. You can create your own units and study them using the Study Zone, based on the spaced repetition method.

What's "spaced repetition" method

It is a learning technique that consists of learning a certain amount of information by allowing longer and longer periods of time between one training session and the next one. It's one of the most recommended to study a language because it improves your long term memory.
